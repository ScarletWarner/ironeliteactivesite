Meet the Coaches

Roman Denton 

J3U NSCA-PT
Competition + Lifestyle Coach

    My name is Roman Denton. I’m a 10+ year Certified Personal Trainer, Youth Sports Certified Coach, Writer for Mountain Dog Diet, the Midwest Sales Manager for ALLMAX Nutrition and now an Online Fitness and Nutrition Coach. I started my fitness journey 15 years ago as an undersized motocross rider with dreams to go to the Pro Circuit. I couldn't lift my bike off the ground due to a lack of overall strength. My Coach at the time suggested I take a weightlifting course and from then on I was hooked. Now with over 12 years working solely in the Fitness and Nutrition Industry, I am expanding my training beyond local clients. I am accredited in many different organizations. I focus on athletes from Aesthetics to Performance & everything in between. Whether your goals are competitive or lifestyle, I will help you reach them. All I expect is you follow the plan, communicate with me at all times, and NEVER give up!

Scarlet Warner

OFNC
Lifestyle Coach

    My name is Scarlet, I'm a certified online fitness and nutrition coach. I spent the majority of my life either obese or overweight, and was obsessed with trying every fad diet I could find to try and lose it. If it was on a cover of a checkout magazine, I probably tried it at some point. It wasn't until I finally learned about proper nutrition and exercise that I started getting actual sustainable results. Now it's my goal to help other people get those type of results. I emphasis a health-focused diet and lifestyle, so you can know the results you'll see will stick around. Whether you want to lose fat or gain muscle, or any combination of both, I can help you get there.
